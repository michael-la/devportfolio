About Me

I'm a 4th year Computer Science student with an interest in cybersecurity and data-driven decision-making. I'm actively seeking a job or internship for the summer of 2024.

While I was studying remotely in 2020, I had the memorable experience of working in IT and computer repair for a company that served both local "mom and pa" businesses, as well as corporations with international influence. Despite the fact that we worked out of a tiny lab in a suburban business district, we had fingers branching out across the country through our remote servers. Since then, I've been fascinated by the way increasingly faster networks and affordable cloud computing have given the power of international scale to anyone with a computer and an internet connection.

I'm also a committed multisport athlete. I'm a varsity rower, and recently earned UCI a gold medal at our national championships. On the off-season, I compete in triathlons, and take to the mountains for rock and alpine climbing.

I am always available for a coffee chat, and I'd love to discuss technology, sports, and more!
